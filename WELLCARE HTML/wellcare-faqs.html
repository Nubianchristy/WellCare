Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the Sign Up button in the header, fill out your details, and verify your email. After that you can log in immediately.

What payment methods are supported?

We accept international remittances via bank/SWIFT, local mobile‑money transfers, and cards through our secure payment gateway.

How do I schedule a home visit?

Navigate to Instant Booking, select Home Visit under Care Type, choose your date, and confirm. You’ll receive live updates once a provider is assigned.

Can I track care delivery in real time?

Yes. After booking, you’ll see live status updates—from provider en route all the way to session complete—in your dashboard.

How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Go to My Bookings in your account, select the appointment, and choose Cancel or Reschedule. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the visit may incur a fee.

Is my personal and medical data secure?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We comply with NDPR and employ strict access controls and regular security audits.
